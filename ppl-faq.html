❓ FAQ & Rules

Pirate Premier League · Everything you need to know · We're not associated with official DLS or First Touch Games

🏆 Match Format
All PPL matches are played on Dream League Soccer (DLS) mobile. Each match is a single-leg game (Main League 2 legged). Results are self-reported by both players and confirmed by the admin. Screenshots of the final score must be posted in the Discord results channel.
If match resuls as draw after 90 minutes, it's finalized as draw. Match can be continued to extra time & penalties but not recognized as "League Format" (hence only result on or before 90 Minutes is considered
📋 League Structure
The PPL runs three leagues per season:
• Main League — top-tier competition
• 2nd League — mid-tier competition
• 3rd League — entry-level competition

A Champions League knockout tournament is held at the end of each season for qualified players across all leagues.
📊 How Stats Are Recorded
After each match, the admin manually records:
Goals Scored · Assists · Goals Conceded

Stats are updated on this website within 24 hours of a match being confirmed. If you notice an error, contact the admin on Discord.
🚫 Disputes & Fair Play
Any disputes must be raised within 48 hours of the match result being posted. Evidence (screenshots/video) is required. The admin's decision is final. Unsporting behaviour may result in a points deduction or ban from the league.
🌐 Universal Time Standard
The official PPL time zone is UTC+00 (Coordinated Universal Time). All match deadlines, season start/end dates, and announcements are posted in UTC. Convert to your local time when scheduling matches with opponents. A live UTC clock is shown on the PPL home page.
📅 Season Schedule
• Season Start — announced on Discord
• Match Deadline — each round has a 7-day window (UTC)
• Playoffs / CL — held in the final 2 weeks of each season
• Trophy Ceremony — Discord announcement after all finals

Always check the Discord #announcements channel for exact dates.
⏰ Scheduling a Match
Contact your opponent via Discord DM or the match thread. Agree on a date and time, then post it in the match channel in UTC format.

Example: "We'll play Saturday 20:00 UTC"

If both players cannot agree on a time before the round deadline, the admin may award a walkover or declare a 0-0 draw.
🔄 Time Zone Converter
Common UTC offsets to help you:

📝 Registration Steps
1. Join the PPL Discord server using the button on the home page.
2. Read the #rules channel carefully.
3. Post your DLS username in #registration.
4. Wait for the admin to confirm your registration.
5. Your player profile will be created on this site after your first match.
✅ Requirements
• Must have Dream League Soccer installed on a mobile device
• Must be an active member of the PPL Discord
• Must be available to play matches within round deadlines
• Must follow fair play rules (no hacks/mods)
🏆 League Trophies

• 🥈 2nd League Champion — highest points in 2nd League
• 🥉 3rd League Champion — highest points in 3rd League
• 🌟 Champions League Winner — knockout tournament champion
👟 Individual Awards
• 👟 Golden Boot — most goals scored across all leagues in the season
• 🎯 Playmaker Award — most assists across all leagues in the season

Awards are announced at the end of each season in Discord.

• Win → 3 points
• Draw → 1 point
• Loss → 0 points

Tiebreakers: Goal Difference → Goals Scored → Head-to-Head
