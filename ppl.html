🏴‍☠️ About the League

Pirate Premier League (PPL) is a DLS (Dream League Soccer) mobile football community hosted on Discord. Tournaments are conducted seasonally. Player stats are tracked and updated manually by the league admin. Join the server to participate in the next season!
